## üîÑ THE "ACKNOWLEDGE, REFERENCE, PIVOT" METHOD

## üö® RED FLAG RESPONSES TO AVOID

---

## ‚ùå NEVER SAY THESE:
- "We don't have competitors" (shows naivety)
- "Our projections are conservative" (investors hate this)
- "We're better than Stripe" (arrogant, show don't tell)
- "It's complicated" (if you can't explain it, you don't understand it)
- "We'll figure it out later" (shows lack of planning)
- "Everyone will want this" (never true)
- "We have no risk" (delusional)

---

## ‚úÖ ALWAYS SAY THESE:
- "That's a great question" (buys time, shows confidence)
- "Let me give you a specific example" (concrete vs abstract)
- "Here's what we're seeing in our data" (evidence-based)
- "We're focused on X because..." (strategic thinking)
- "What are your thoughts on that?" (engages them)
- "That's something we've thought about..." (shows preparation)
